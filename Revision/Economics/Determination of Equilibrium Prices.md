Excess Demand ^OxVszaVU

At Q2, price is at P2, which is below market equilibrium. Demand is now greater than 
supply, which can be calculated by Q3 - Q2. This is a state of disequilibrium - the 
demand price does not equal the supply price, and the quantity demanded does not 
equal the quantity supplied. This is a shortage in the market, which pushes prices 
up and causes firms to supply more. Since prices increase, demand will contract. Once 
supply meets demand again, price will reach the market clearing price, P1. ^CiXwAxMw
